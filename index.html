Violence is the shape of god.

Bleeding gums weep rancid pink muck or grime which I slosh about in playfully as a pig or other beast in the throes of joy perhaps induced perhaps produced but joy none the less. and the sky is neon red hexadecimal ff0000 encasing a turquoise sun like blood on the floor of a catacomb. and the grinning tombstones look so, so, like teeth but that cannot be as i am here to eat god but god will eat me. death is a test i cannot pass and i hold on to my rotting ego.
